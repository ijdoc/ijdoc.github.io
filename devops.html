Harmony Through Automation

As Senior DevOps Engineer at Peraso, I used iPXE, Python scripts, and a service backend, to automate monitoring and recovery of testing setups for the company's WiGig chipsets, reducing failure downtime/recovery from days to minutes.

The implementation also helped improve relations between Engineering departments, not only due to the performance improvement, but to the normalization of mistakes as a natural part of the development process, since failures no longer had a significant impact on core operations.

DevOps Gives You Wings.

As CTO of tecla (2010 - 2018) I implemented the continuous integration (CI) and continuous development (CD) pipelines that enabled a handful of Engineers to test, maintain and deploy two mobile apps (Android and iOS) as well as multiple OTA firmware releases for our flagship device.

Without these pipelines, it would have been impossible to run such a lean operation.
