Proud teams deliver

Above all, managers must mitigate the obstacles threatening the team's success, and enable team members to take ownership and pride in their craft.

Rolling up our sleeves.

As CTO of tecla (2010 - 2018) I was responsible for the recruitment, management, coordination and mentorship of a team of consultants, designers, engineers, manufacturers and suppliers, distributed across three continents. Together we drove three development life cycles for the tecla product line, including the ideation, planning, prototyping, implementation, testing, commercial launch, customer support, maintenance and graceful degradation of a consumer electronic device, two Android apps and one iOS app.

In addition to management, I personally made fundamental hands-on contributions to user experience design, wireframing, systems architecture, devops, embedded (firmware) development, Android app development, and IT administration.
